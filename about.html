About Us

RK Hardware & Construction
Bulawayo, Zimbabwe

A proudly woman-run company serving Bulawayo’s contractors, builders, and homeowners since 1999.

Leghorn Investments T/A RK Hardware and Construction was established in 1999 to fill a critical gap in the construction and hardware supplies sector in Bulawayo, Zimbabwe.

Founded on three enduring principles — operational excellence, quality products, and superior customer service — we have served Bulawayo for over two decades. We supply government bodies, NGOs, private developers, and homeowners across Zimbabwe.

As a proudly woman-run company, we champion women’s empowerment in the construction industry while upholding the highest standards of honesty, integrity, and quality.
